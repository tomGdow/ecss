  1. The WebVTT (Web Video Text Tracks) format is intended for marking up external text track resources in connection with with the track element
  2. WebVTT files provide
    1. captions
    2. subtitles
    3. text video descriptions
    4. chapters for content navigation
    5. any form of metadata that is time-aligned with audio or video content
